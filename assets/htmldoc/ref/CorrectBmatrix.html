CorrectBmatrix

CorrectBmatrix[bmat, transformation]
corrects the bmatrix bmat with the tranformation parameters from RegisterData or RegisterDiffusionData. Output is the corrected bmatrix.

DetailsDetails

  • The following options can be given:
  • MethodRegFullMethodReg is an options for RegisterData, RegisterDiffusionData, RegisterCardiacData and RegisterDataTransform. It spefifies which registration method to use. Mehtods can be be "translation", "rigid", "affine", "bspline", "rigidDTI", "affineDTI", "PCAtranslation", "PCArigid", "PCAaffine", or "PCAbspline".
