QMRITools PACKAGE SYMBOL

FitTract

FitTract[tract]
fits a tract defined as a list of {x,y,z} coordinates with a polinomial function.

FitTract[{tract, …}]
fits a list of tracts defined as a list of {x,y,z} coordinates with a polinomial function.
