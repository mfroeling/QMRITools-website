ImportDTI

ImportDTI[folder]
imports xx.dat, yy.dat, zz.dat, xy.dat, xz.dat and yz.dat from the given folder.

ImportDTI[folder, add]
imports xx-add.dat, yy-add.dat, zz-add.dat, xy-add.dat, xz-add.dat and yz-add.dat from the given folder.

ImportDTI[{file$1, file$2, ..}]
imports the given *.dat files.
