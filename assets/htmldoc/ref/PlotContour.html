PlotContour

PlotContour[data, vox]
creates a contour of the data.

PlotContour[data, vox, scale]
creates a contour of the data with the surface colored acording to scale.

PlotContour[data, vox, scale, range]
creates a contour of the data with the surface colored acording to scale with a fixed plotrange.

DetailsDetails

  • The following options can be given:
  • ContourStyle{GrayLevel[0.5], 0.25}ContourStyle is an option for contour plots that specifies the style in which contour lines or surfaces should be drawn.
