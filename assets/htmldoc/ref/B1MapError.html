B1MapError

B1MapError[chan$1, chan$2, mask, {f, phase, scale}, TR, alpha]
calculates the root mean square error of the b1 with a target of 100% within the mask.

B1MapError[chan$1, chan$2, mask, target, {f, phase, scale}, TR, alpha]
calculates the root mean square error of the b1 with target within the mask.
