TractographyTools

QMRITools package: Contains functions for fiber tractography and fiber analysis.

ReferenceReference

FiberTractography — performs fibertractography on the tensor.

FindTensorPermutation — performs tractography for all tensor permutations and gives back the one that has the longest tracts. (10.1016/j.media.2014.05.012)

FitTract — fits a tract with a polynomial function.
