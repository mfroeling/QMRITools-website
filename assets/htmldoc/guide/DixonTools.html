DixonTools

QMRITools package: Functions that are used for Dixon data and reconstruction.

ReferenceReference

DixonReconstruct — Performs Dixon reconstruction of real and imaginary data with B0 and T2* corrections (10.1002/mrm.20624 and 10.1002/mrm.21737).

DixonToPercent — Calculates the water and fat fractions from magnitude or complex water and fat images.

Unwrap — Fast 2D and 3D phase unwrapping (10.1364/AO.46.006623 and 10.1364/AO.41.007437)
