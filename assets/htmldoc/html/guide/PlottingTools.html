PlottingTools

QMRITools package. Functions for displaying and visualization of multi dimensional data.

PlotData — Plots 2D 3D and 4D data. One ore two datasets, adding legends, changing colors, adding labels, etc.

PlotData3D — Plots 3D data in a 3D data viewer WIP.

PlotSegmentations — Plots a 3D surface render of segmentations.

GradientPlot — Plots the diffusion gradients.
