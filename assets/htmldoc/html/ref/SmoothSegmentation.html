SmoothSegmentation[masks]

smooths segmentations and removes the overlaps between multiple segmentations.

Details

  • The following options can be given:
  • MaskFiltKernel2MaskFiltKernel is an option for Mask, SmoothMask and SmoothSegmentation. How mucht the contours are smoothed.
    MaskComponents0MaskComponents is an option for Mask and SmoothMask. Determinse the amount of largest clusters used as mask.
