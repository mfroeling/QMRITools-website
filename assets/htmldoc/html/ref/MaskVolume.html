MaskVolume
SegmentationVolume[mask] calculates the volume of the mask. SegmentationVolume[{mask, ..}] calculates the Median volume of the List of masks. SegmentationVolume[mask, vox] calculates the volume of the mask in cm^3 where vox is in mm. SegmentationVolume[{mask, ..}, vox] calculates the Median volume of the List of masks in cm^3 where vox is in mm.
