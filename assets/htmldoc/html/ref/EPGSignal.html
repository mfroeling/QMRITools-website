EPGSignal

EPGSignal[{Necho,echoSpace},{T$1,T$2},{ex_angle,ref_angle},B$1]

generates a EPG T2 curve with stimulated echos. T1, T2 and echoSpace are in ms, angel is in degree, B1 is between 0 and 1. Output is the EPG Signal vector.

EPGSignal[]

is based on DOI: 10.1002/jmri.24619.
