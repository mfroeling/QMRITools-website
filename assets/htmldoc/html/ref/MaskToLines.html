MaskToLines

MaskToLines[mask,vox]

calculates lines perpendicular to the heart wall per slice within the mask. Internally it uses CalculateWallMap and CentralAxes to obtain the cardiac geometry from mask.

MaskToLines[mask,wall,cent]

where mask is the first output of CalculateWallMap and cent is the first output of CentralAxes.
