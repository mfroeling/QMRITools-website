Tensor

Tensor[{l1, l2, l3}] creates a diffuison tensor with vectors {{0,0,1},{0,1,0},{1,0,0}} and eigenvalues {l1, l2, l3}. Tensor[{l1, l2, l3}, {e1, e2, e3}] creates a diffuison tensor with vectors {e1, e2, e3} and eigenvalues {l1, l2, l3}. Tensor[{l1, l2, l3}, "Random"] creates a diffuison tensor with random orthogonal eigenvectors {e1, e2, e2} and eigenvalues {l1, l2, l3}. Tensor[{l1, l2, l3}, "RandomZ"] creates a diffuison tensor with random orthogonal eigenvectors {{1,0,0}, e2, e3} with random eigenvectors and eigenvalues {l1, l2, l3}. Tensor[{l1, l2, l3}, "OrtRandom"] creates a diffuison tensor with random orthogonal eigenvectors {{1,0,0},{0,1,0},{0,0,1}} and eigenvalues {{l1l1, l2, l3l3}}. Tensor[] is based on DOI: 10.1002/nbm.2959.
