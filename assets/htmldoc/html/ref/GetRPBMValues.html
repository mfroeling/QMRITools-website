GetRPBMValues

GetRPBMValues[{d0,tau,zeta}]

derives parameters from RPBM function. d0, tau and zeta are the parameters of the RPBM function. Ouput is a list containing {{d0d0, tau, zeta, dinf, td, tr, I, sv, a, kappa}.
