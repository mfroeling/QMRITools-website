GradientCoilTensor

GradientCoilTensor[mask,vox,off,dint]

makes the gradient coil tensor from the mask, with the resolution defined by vox. The offset off is the left most vox coordinate in the scanner coordinate systeme and the gradient tensor dint comes from the function MakeGradientDerivatives.
