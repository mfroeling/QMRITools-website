SequenceTSE

SequenceTSE[din ,ham,{te,necho},{ex,ref}]

performs a multi echo spin echo experiment with echo time te with necho echos of the spin system din given the hamiltonian ham using ex Degree exitation and ref Degree refocus pulses.

SequenceTSE[din ,ham,{te,necho},{ex,ref},b1]

performs a multi echo spin echo experiment with echo time te with necho echos of the spin system din given the hamiltonian ham using ex Degree exitation and ref Degree refocus pulses and b1. The te is defined in ms, the ex and ref are defined in degree and b1 of 100% is defined as 1. The output is a new spinsystem dout.
